HOW DOES IT WORK?


Download

When launched, you simply download from the BlackBox Home Page to your device and install as with any mobile application. Our application will only be available for download through us, to ensure that our software is not breached or "back doored" by any third parties before it reaches your device.

Add Unique ID's or Numbers

Add a number, unique ID or a group of either and go. As long as your recipients also have the BlackBox application, the communication will be secure.

Connect

BlackBox randomly scrambles your communications into many little bit's of encrypted data, only the intended recipient's have the key's to de-crypt incoming messages and communications. There are many levels of encryption working togather to ensure that even if your data is intercepted, it is nearly impossible to decipher it.
