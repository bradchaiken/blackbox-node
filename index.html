The Technology


Talk

BlackBox will provide secure talk among all cellular carriers as well as all major hardware manufacturers. We are currently developing applications for Android, IOS and Windows devices, with more targeted in the very near future.

Text

Our application also allows for completely secure text and Instant Messaging over an encrypted network, giving you confidence in all of your communications.

Privacy

Account set up's are not limited to name and email. This allows for complete anonymous communication between you and your party. Your data is never stored by us or any other party.

Open Source

We have absolutely nothing to hide. Want to see the source code? Have a look. We are that confident in our product. Most of all, we believe that everyone should have the right to secure communications.
